Banking Credit Risk Analytics Dashboard 💳📊

Turning Raw Banking Data into Business Intelligence

This project showcases a professional Power BI Banking Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze customer behavior,
monitor financial activity, detect fraud risks, and calculate customer credit scores using real-world business 
logic and DAX calculations.

Built with interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and a custom credit scoring model,
this dashboard simulates how modern banks evaluate customer
financial health and operational performance.

📌 Business Problem

Banks manage thousands of customer transactions daily.
Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to:

Identify risky customers

Monitor loan performance

Detect fraud activity

Evaluate customer creditworthiness

Track transaction trends

Improve financial decision-making

This project solves these problems through data analytics and visualization.

🎯 Key Insights

Customers with low credit utilization usually maintain higher credit scores.

Fraud records significantly reduce customer financial trustworthiness.

High-value loans increase customer financial risk.

Customer transaction behavior helps identify banking trends and risk patterns.

Credit score segmentation improves loan approval decisions.
